Hello, my name is Kelsey Mack and I'd like to tell you a little about me.

I'm from a small town called Rome, Georgia and I'm 32 years old (33 on July 26th). I have one older sister and a younger sister and brother. My dad is a state trooper and still lives in Georgia with my younger siblings. My mom is an assistant principal in Texas where we moved when I was in the 10th grade. Once I graduated high school, I went to college for a semester but couldn't decide what I wanted to do, so in 2012 I made the decision to join the Navy. I was an IT while in and enjoyed learning new things, seeing new places and making friends that turned in to family.

Once I got out, I moved back to Texas for two years to figure out what my next step in life should be.

After those two years I moved to Charlotte, so back to the East Coast we go! I moved here in 2017. I didn't know anyone and my closest family is five hours away. It was definitely an interesting time to navigate through, but I had a three year old and another child on the way so, I figured it out.
